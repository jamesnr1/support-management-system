Privacy Policy

Support Management System

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Information We Collect

Our Support Management System is designed to help manage support worker schedules and participant care plans. We collect and process the following information:

  • Worker Information: Names, contact details, qualifications, and availability
  • Participant Information: Names, care requirements, and support schedules
  • Schedule Data: Work shifts, assignments, and roster information
  • Calendar Data: With your permission, we access your Google Calendar to display appointments alongside schedules

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage support worker schedules
  • Track participant care plans and requirements
  • Generate reports and maintain records
  • Display calendar appointments when you choose to connect Google Calendar

Google Calendar Integration

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar:

  • We use read-only access to display your calendar appointments
  • We do not create, modify, or delete any calendar events
  • Your calendar data is only accessed when you explicitly authorize it
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for Google Calendar access

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
